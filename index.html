Lodging:

There will be room for up to 25 guests at the main estate. This will largely be reserved for family and members of the Wedding Party, though we'd love to fit as many people as possible. We'll reach out via email to coordinate this.

There are also many other lodging options in the area, including AirBnB, Inns, and the beautiful Bullfrog Pond Campground. Be sure to reserve early (even the campground!) as spots may fill up. We should be able to arrange camping gear for those flying in.

The Town:

Guerneville is a sweet, small town in Sonoma County. There's a grocery store, cafe, pub, and a bunch of local art vendors. The Russian River runs through the town and is great for swimming and/or kayaking. There's a lot of beautiful hiking with diverse terrains and views of the ocean -- which also happens to be just a short drive away. :)
