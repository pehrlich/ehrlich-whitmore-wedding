~ Venue & Schedule ~

  • Saturday
    • Morning: Pancake Breakfast
    • Midday: Ceremony in Back Yard

    • Evening: Dance at the House

Main Venue:

There will be room for up to 25 guests at the Enchanted Forest vacation rental. This is largely reserved for family and members of the Wedding Party, though we'd love to fit as many people as possible.

The Town:

Guerneville is a sweet, small town in Sonoma County. There's a grocery store, cafe, pub, and a bunch of local art vendors. The Russian River runs through the town and is great for swimming and/or kayaking. There's a lot of beautiful hiking with diverse terrains and views of the ocean -- which also happens to be just a short drive away. :)

~ Transportation ~

You'll need a car to reach our venue. It's about a 1.5 hr drive from San Francisco.

If flying, we recommend arriving at San Francisco International Airport (SFO) or Oakland International Airport (OAK). Our venue is a 1.5 - 2 hr drive from either of these airports. We also recommend checking flights arriving at Santa Rosa Airport (STS). This is a smaller airport and is a quick 20 minute drive to our venue.

Carpool if you can -- parking may be limited! If you have extra seats or need a ride, please fill out the Carpool Information Form for one of these locations.

Will food be provided through the weekend?

In addition to the Saturday catered reception, we will be providing food for the entire weekend in the form of simple breakfasts and lunches, and home-cooked meals. There is also a Safeway nearby and plenty of local restaurants if you want to mix things up!

At our venue, we'll be asking for volunteers to help prepare the meals (along with other tasks such as helping decorate, or clean up). There will be a sign-up sheet posted so people can choose how/when they'd like to help!

What should I wear?

We will be having a festive outdoor ceremony and reception, so dress up in something elegant and fun that you love! For the rest of the weekend, just plan for warm days (average highs are 82°), breezy nights, and don't forget flip flops/water shoes and a bathing suit for the river and hot tub, plus shoes for hiking on Sunday.

Should I bring a gift?

We'd appreciate any contribution you'd like to make, whether that be a thoughtful gift, a piece of advice, or a helping hand throughout the weekend. If you need any ideas, our registry is available on Zola https://www.zola.com/registry/robinandpeter
